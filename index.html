Air Traffic Control

Air Traffic Control being at the epicentre of activities in the aviation industry receives a lot of signal interference and hence delays in flights. The cumilative cost of this delay is estimated to be more than a billion globally. Currently, ATC companies, have bought several communication systems and several infrastructure to work with the problem rather than bbeing proactive to efficiently solve the problem. Such a scenario also puts the communication system at risk of hacking and listening. Using our state of the art solution, the problem will not only be identified when it occurs, but it will be identified even before it causes any serious damage. Our system can also predict the source of the problem immediately assuring complete safety and clear communication to the flights

Defense and Space

There are several use cases for Defense related projects where our system is of high interest. One of the main projects that we are developing is the no-cross talk zone project where in highly secure areas, constant communication of several systems take place but it is highly important to know whether these communications are authorised or not. If this communication not intended to happen or someone is communicating without proper authorisation, such transmissions can be easily monitored, classified and notified to the authorities. This system is also linked with other monitoring systems to provide a clear picture of the complete activities happening in an area.

Radiation Level Monitoring

In 21st century, every person has on an average 2 devices that continuosuly communicate. When mobile phones are left aside, even in their idle state, they are continuosly communicating hence increasing the radiation whenever there is a gathering of a group of people. Our solution, helps common people to analyse the radiation at a given place at a given time and to contribute to reduce the radiation level or to avoid such crowded areas in order to ensure good health and to lead a better life.
